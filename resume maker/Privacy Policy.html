Welcome to the AI Resume Maker App by Code Solix. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding that information. By using the app you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the app:

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the resume creation features and templates.

  • Deliver customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Analyze usage to improve templates, UI/UX, and performance, and to diagnose technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. AI Processing & Resume Content

The app's AI features (resume scoring and suggestions) analyze the resume content you provide. Resume content is processed to produce the score and tailored recommendations. This processing is necessary for the app’s core functionality.

By default, resume data you enter is stored locally on your device. If you choose to enable cloud backup, synchronization, or premium features that require server-side processing, your resume data may be securely transmitted to and stored on our servers or trusted third-party service providers to provide those features.
